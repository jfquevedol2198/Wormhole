Wormhole Introduction

Wormhole is a revolutionary new portfolio tracking software, engineered specifically to tackle the new pricing and tracking issues figuresented by DeFi investments.

While administrators and tax professionals have largely solved the crypto issues raised by CeFi, DeFi is unsolved. Presently, administrators typically handle DeFi composite transactions, airdrops, and derivative transactions with spreadsheet technology.

This results in arbitrary pricing that often canâ€™t be replicated independently and there is no systematic chain tracking of component investments.

Wormhole enables administrators and tax professionals to achieve 100% verifiable on chain tracking and historical derivative pricing across 90 DeFi protocols, which represent 95% of the active market, thus bringing the administrator role in DeFi in line with the replicability and accuracy of other asset classes. Wormhole can track and price any DeFi wallet address in an independently replicable process on a current or historical basis.

Team and Market

Wormhole was created by a team of 10 developers brought together last year by Jason Stone, one of the single largest DeFi investors.

We run 2 archive nodes which are full independent copies of the history of the blockchain that enables our tracking and categorization of blockchain transactions.

The team consists of 4 blockchain engineers and 6 front end engineers with a combined 30 years of blockchain programming experience, dating back to the first days of crypto.

DeFi is the fastest growing part of crypto and that is expected to continue to eclipse large parts of CeFi over the medium term.

However, many larger investors have remained on the sidelines in part because of the tracking issues and subjective valuations created with the shift from CeFi to DeFi. Wormhole solves this.
